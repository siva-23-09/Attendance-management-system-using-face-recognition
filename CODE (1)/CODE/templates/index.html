Facial recognition:

Facial recognition—a type of biometric technology—mimics how people identify or verify others by examining their faces. Recent advancements have increased the accuracy of automated FRT resulting in increased use across a range of applications.

Benefits

There are many benefits facial recognition can offer society, from preventing crimes and increasing safety and security to reducing unnecessary human interaction and labor.

Services

A facial recognition system is a technology capable of matching a human face from a digital image or a video frame against a database of faces, typically employed to authenticate users through ID verification services, works by pinpointing and measuring facial features from a given image.

Uses

Place a kiosk (tablet or mobile) device at the entrance. Student simply walk towards it to trigger face recognition and record attendance.
